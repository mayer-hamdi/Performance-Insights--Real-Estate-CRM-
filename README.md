## üîç The Core Problems
While working directly with clients and managing high-volume sales pipelines, I consistently encountered two systemic issues that reduce team efficiency and agent growth:

1.  **The Feedback Gap in Sales Performance:** There was no systematic way for sales agents to receive feedback on *where* in the funnel they were losing opportunities. If a lead they worked on was rotated and later converted by another agent, that critical learning data was lost, making improvement guesswork.
2.  **The Daily Planning Disconnect:** Daily task management was fragmented. Agents and managers lacked a unified, time-aware view of priorities, leading to misalignment, missed follow-ups, and a lack of structured daily workflow.

**Solution Validation:** These problems were qualitatively validated through discussions with multiple peers in similar roles, confirming they were team-wide challenges needing a tool-based solution.

---

## üõ†Ô∏è The Proposed Solution
I designed an integrated suite of two features that work within a CRM dashboard to close these gaps:

### 1. Performance Insights Dashboard
-   **Purpose:** Transform lost opportunities into learning moments.
-   **Core Concept:** Introduces a **"Stage-Specific Opportunity Loss Rate"** metric. It visually answers: "When I advance a lead to the Presentation stage, what percentage convert later with someone else?"
-   **Value:** Enables precise, data-driven coaching and self-assessment for sales agents.

### 2. Collaborative Daily Planner
-   **Purpose:** Create alignment and structure in daily execution.
-   **Core Features:**
    -   **Time-Blocked Schedule:** Tasks are organized in a hourly timeline, not just a list.
    -   **Shared Task Management:** Both agents and managers can create, assign, and comment on tasks.
    -   **Daily Recap & Notes:** A dedicated section for end-of-day summaries and setting next-day priorities, ensuring continuous alignment.
